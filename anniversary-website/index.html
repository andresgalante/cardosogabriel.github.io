Bhakti Marga's Just Love Festival

"Love changes everything."

10-12 July 2015 at Springen (Frankfurt), Germany
Previous Next
"Just Love! Love is the greatest worship." Sri Swami Vishwananda

Just Love. Love is all there is. Love is all you have to do. The more you love, the more you reflect it and the more you will discover yourself.

We all know how it is to fall in Love, but by directing our Love towards the Self we can actually rise in Love. We can experience a Love which goes beyond our imagination.

The Just Love festival is dedicated to "rising" in Love. Through the music, spiritual discourses and meditation we don't "fall" in Love with the outside but we "rise" in Love inside. Take part in the festival, transform yourself and rise in Love.

The first ever Just Love Festival coincides with the 10 year anniversary of Bhakti Marga. There will be some special activities and programs to celebrate the occasion.

The festival will be taking place at the Shree Peetha Nilaya international centre. Located in the heart of Germany, Shree Peetha Nilaya means the “Abode of the Divine Mother,” a place where you can leave your worries behind. It is the home of Sri Swami Vishwananda and the Bhakti Marga Community which was founded by him in 2005.

Bhakti means Love and devotion, while Marga stands for path. It is the path to the heart. Everyone is welcome, and no one that comes leaves empty handed. Join us at Shree Peetha Nilaya:

The Home of Bhakti!

Everyone is Welcome.

The festival is free of charge and open to everyone. Don’t miss the opportunity to take part in the festival and to grow in Love.
