“Just Love! Love is the greatest worship.”

Sri Swami Vishwananda

Sri Swami Vishwananda is delighted to invite you to join him on 10-12 July for a three-day celebration of the tenth anniversary of his mission in this life. For Guruji, this inaugural festival celebration is our most important event of the year. Opening with Guru Puja and a journey back through the highlights of our international Bhakti Marga family growth, Guruji will set out his vision for our future journey together into this new era of consciousness.

We will enjoy three nights of world renowned musical performances, a full range of Bhakti Marga activities, presentations and satsang during the day, a buzzing array of market stalls, plus fresh vegetarian indian and western food stalls to keep you sustained and nourished.

This festival environment provides the perfect opportunity to bring along friends or family who have never been to Shree Peetha Nilaya, the international home of Bhakti Marga. We will have a wonderful celebration with three unforgettable days and nights with Guruji, so make your travel plans now. Call your friends, register, book your accommodations and get ready for the party of the year!

Please don’t miss this opportunity to honour Guruji and be among family as we celebrate the power of Love to change everything.

The 10 year anniversary celebration will be taking place at the Shree Peetha Nilaya international centre. It is the home of Sri Swami Vishwananda and the Bhakti Marga Community which was founded by him in 2005.

Shree Peetha Nilaya

It is in Germany that Bhakti Marga has its headquarters. In the beautiful countryside of the Tanus region lies Shree Peetha Nilaya (“abode of the Divine Mother”). The Centre and Ashram is the main hub of Bhakti Marga and the work of Swamiji.

It’s a place of nonstop spirituality. Residents and guests come together daily to participate in prayers, meditation, singing, Seva and to share their Love of the Divine with one another. Large festivals are held monthly with participants from around the world; creating a dynamic atmosphere of transformation and spiritual development that provides each seeker with what they are searching for.
